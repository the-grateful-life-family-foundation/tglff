About The Grateful Life Family Foundation

Discover our mission to help humans be better people by changing the world, one mind at a time

Our Mission

To help humans be better people by changing the world, one mind at a time. We achieve this by providing tools and methodologies to raise awareness of others' needs, elevating the planet's collective consciousness.

Our Philosophy

  • We are 'one' and our actions affect others
  • Every person has esteemed value, assuming good intentions
  • Seeking reconciliation through forgiveness
  • Co-creating love and inclusivity
  • Collective consciousness is our source and supply

Our Location

Lake Waldensia is a 372-acre wooded property in Ozone, TN, serving as a nomadic haven and a place for tranquility, adventure, and connection. Located on Millstone Mountain Road, Rockwood, TN 37854, visitors can explore local history and culture while connecting with nature.

Trail of Trees

"Rooted in remembrance, the Trail of Trees honors those who walked the Trail of Tears by planting a living legacy across the lands they once crossed."

Beginning in Ozone, Tennessee, at The Grateful Life Family Foundation, saplings serve as symbols of healing, unity, and life.

"Every tree tells a story. Every step revives a memory. Every donation plants a future."

Our Purpose

  • Turning pain into purpose
  • Cultural remembrance
  • Climate regeneration

Native Trees We Plant

  • Red Cedar - for ecological value and historical significance
  • White Oak - supporting carbon offset
  • Dogwood - for habitat restoration
  • Persimmon - promoting public healing
